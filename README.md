# FitnessPro
FitnessPro is a personal fitness app that allows users to have their personal fitness journey all tracked on one app. This app will provide you not only with wrokouts and good dietary reciepes, but also a way to track your progress and help achieve the goals you set for your self 

Link to project: 

Features:
- Track Workouts: Log your daily workouts including exercises performed, duration, sets, reps, and intensity levels.
- Set Goals: Establish personalized fitness goals and monitor your progress over time to stay motivated and on track.
- Exercise Library: Access a vast variety of exercises with detailed descriptions and instructional videos to ensure proper form and technique.
- Workout Routines: Choose from pre-designed workout routines or create your own custom routines tailored to your specific needs and preferences.
- Schedule Workouts: Plan and schedule your workouts in advance to stay organized and committed to your fitness routine.
- Performance Analytics: Analyze performance metrics and trends to identify areas for improvement and optimize your training regimen.
- Personalized Recommendations: Receive personalized workout recommendations and tips based on your fitness objectives and preferences.
